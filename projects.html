Salesforce Demo Email Capture Automation

Purpose: Auto-populated demo attendee email into Opportunity for HubSpot enrollment.

System: Record-triggered Flow tied to “Client Demo” event creation.

View details

Context: SDRs booked demos via a “Book Demo” button, creating a Salesforce Event labeled “Client Demo” and a synced Google Calendar invite. Leads were converted at booking.

Structural Problem: Demo attendee email started on the Lead, moved to Contact after conversion, but marketing required it in the Opportunity field Newsletter Email to sync to HubSpot.

Architecture Built:

  • Record-Triggered Flow in Salesforce.
  • Triggered when a “Client Demo” event was created.
  • Copied Contact email into Opportunity Newsletter Email field.
  • Field synced to HubSpot and enrolled prospect in post-demo campaign.

Impact: Removed manual email copying and reduced missed marketing enrollments.
