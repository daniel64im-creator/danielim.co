About

I began my career in sales and transitioned toward Revenue Operations after repeatedly identifying structural inefficiencies inside go-to-market systems. Much of my work has focused on improving CRM governance, enforcing process consistency, and designing automation that reduces operational friction across revenue teams.

While working inside SaaS organizations, I began building operational systems that improved visibility, reduced manual work, and strengthened the integrity of CRM data. These systems included governance frameworks, workflow automations, enrichment pipelines, and reporting infrastructure used by SDR teams and sales leadership.

My focus is on designing scalable operational architecture rather than one-off fixes. Most of the projects in this portfolio document real operational problems and the systems designed to solve them.

I am currently pursuing Revenue Operations and Sales Operations Analyst roles where I can continue building systems that improve CRM integrity, automation reliability, and revenue team visibility.
