Customer Allocation & Cross-Sell Governance

Governance System
Governance - Account Ownership - SDR Operations - Salesforce

Operational governance framework designed to control expansion behavior across a multi-product sales organization. The system defined ownership rules, protected active opportunities, and reduced duplicate outreach risk across shared accounts.

System Overview

The sales organization operated across multiple product lines targeting the same institutional customer base. Accounts could have simultaneous activity across different products, creating ambiguity around expansion ownership and outreach permissions. This project introduced a structured allocation and governance framework to control how teams interacted with shared accounts.

Structural Gap

  • No formal ownership model governing expansion activity.
  • Contacts involved in active opportunities were not protected.
  • Cross-product outreach created duplicate outreach risk.
  • Manual handoffs introduced visibility gaps between teams.
  • Ownership disputes slowed SDR execution.

Architecture

  • Ownership Tier Model - Defined clear expansion rights by role and product alignment.
  • Account Hold States - Introduced temporary hold classifications preventing simultaneous outreach.
  • Cross-Product Guardrails - Enabled Product B outreach while protecting contacts tied to Product A opportunities.
  • Reassignment Protocol - Structured handoff process maintaining visibility and account history.
  • Decision Logic Documentation - Documented allowable outreach conditions to eliminate subjective interpretation.
Governance Workflow Account Activity Detected - Ownership Tier Evaluation - Contact Protection Check - Outreach Allowed or Blocked - Reassignment Path (if required)

Tools

  • Salesforce CRM
  • SDR allocation tracking systems
  • Governance documentation frameworks
  • Manual conflict resolution workflows

Scale

  • Users impacted: SDR team + sales leadership
  • Environment: multi-product SaaS sales organization
  • Primary objective: prevent duplicate outreach and protect active deals

Operational Impact

  • Reduced internal friction during expansion workflows.
  • Lowered duplicate outreach incidents across product teams.
  • Improved clarity around expansion ownership.
  • Strengthened operational discipline across shared accounts.

Future Improvements

  • Automate conflict detection across open opportunities.
  • Introduce audit trail for ownership changes.
  • Surface expansion eligibility indicators on account layouts.
