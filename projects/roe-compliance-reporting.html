Reporting system designed to surface outreach eligibility under Rules of Engagement (ROE) policies, allowing managers and SDRs to quickly identify which accounts and contacts were compliant for outreach activity.

System Overview

Rules of Engagement policies defined when outreach was permitted based on existing opportunities, account ownership, and contact participation in active deals. However, enforcement relied on manual review and tribal knowledge, making consistent compliance difficult to maintain.

This project introduced reporting views that clearly surfaced outreach eligibility and highlighted records at risk of violating governance rules.

Structural Gap

  • No centralized view of outreach eligibility.
  • Manual compliance checks slowed SDR workflow.
  • Managers lacked reliable audit visibility.
  • Edge cases required time-consuming investigation.
