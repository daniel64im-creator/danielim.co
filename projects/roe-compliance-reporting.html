CRM Outreach Eligibility & ROE Compliance Reporting

Reporting layer that made outreach eligibility and ROE compliance visible without manual auditing.

Context

  • ROE rules required consistent enforcement across accounts, opportunities, and contacts.
  • Managers needed fast visibility into compliance and risk areas.

Structural Problems

  • Compliance checks were manual and inconsistent.
  • Reps lacked clarity on “eligible vs. not eligible” targets.
  • Leadership lacked a repeatable view for audits and coaching.

Architecture Built

  • Eligibility Definitions: Codified what qualifies a record for outreach under ROE rules.
  • Compliance Views: Reports/dashboards to surface violations, exceptions, and edge cases.
  • Operational Workflow: Made the reporting usable for daily coaching and team review.
  • Risk Surfacing: Highlighted accounts/contacts with active opportunity protections.

Governance & Controls

  • Created a single reference view for enforcement decisions.
  • Reduced “interpretation variance” between managers and reps.
  • Improved auditability without slowing down execution.

Impact

  • Improved ROE adherence by making eligibility visible.
  • Reduced manager time spent manually investigating edge cases.
  • Increased confidence in outreach behavior across products.

Next Improvements

  • Automate exception logging when violations are detected.
  • Add scheduled summary notifications for high-risk changes.
  • Extend coverage to additional edge-case scenarios.
