Executive Customer Advisory Targeting

Strategic Customer Segmentation System

Targeting and segmentation framework used to identify high-fit institutions for participation in an executive-level customer advisory initiative.

System Overview

Executive advisory initiatives require carefully selected participants to ensure productive discussions and strategic relevance. Prior invite lists were often created through informal recommendations or incomplete data, making the process difficult to justify and replicate.

This project introduced a structured targeting workflow that applied consistent inclusion criteria and produced a validated invite list for leadership review.

Structural Gap

  • Invite lists were built through informal suggestions.
  • Targeting decisions lacked transparent criteria.
  • Data quality inconsistencies reduced confidence.
  • No repeatable system for future advisory cycles.

Architecture

  • Selection Criteria - Defined strategic inclusion factors such as institutional fit, engagement history, and product relevance.
  • Segmentation Workflow - Process used to compile candidate institutions across relevant segments.
  • Deduplication Layer - Removed duplicate institutions and redundant contacts.
  • Stakeholder Review Output - Final validated invite list delivered for executive approval.

Tools

  • CRM customer datasets
  • Segmentation and filtering workflows

Scale

  • Primary users: leadership and program stakeholders
  • Scope: executive advisory program targeting
  • Objective: produce high-fit participant invite list

Operational Impact

  • Improved transparency in invite selection.
  • Reduced risk of inappropriate or redundant invitations.
  • Created repeatable targeting workflow for future programs.
  • Strengthened stakeholder confidence in invite quality.

Future Improvements
