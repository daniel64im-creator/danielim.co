Executive Customer Advisory Targeting

Targeting and segmentation system to build a high-fit invite list for executive customer advisory outreach.

Context

  • Need: identify high-fit customers/prospects for an executive-facing advisory initiative.
  • Targeting required clean segmentation and consistent inclusion logic.

Structural Problems

  • Invite lists were ad hoc and difficult to justify.
  • Data quality gaps reduced confidence in targeting decisions.
  • No repeatable system for future advisory cycles.

Architecture Built

  • Selection Criteria: Defined inclusion logic (fit, engagement, segment, strategic relevance).
  • Segmentation Workflow: Built a process to compile and validate targets consistently.
  • Deduplication: Prevented duplicate invites and reduced list noise.
  • Operational Output: Produced a clean, reviewable invite list for stakeholders.

Governance & Controls

  • Criteria-based selection improved transparency.
  • Repeatable workflow reduced reliance on memory and opinion.
  • Validation pass reduced embarrassing invite errors.

Impact

  • Higher confidence in invite list quality and relevance.
  • Improved stakeholder alignment through transparent criteria.
  • Created a reusable targeting system for future cycles.

Next Improvements

  • Add scoring model to rank invite priority.
  • Track acceptance and attendance outcomes for iteration.
  • Automate list refresh and change tracking.
