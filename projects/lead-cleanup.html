Conference Lead Cleanup & Redistribution

Process to convert raw event lead lists into usable, owned outreach queues.

Problem

  • Event leads arrived as raw lists and were not aligned to product interest or team ownership.
  • Without cleanup and routing, follow-up was slow and inconsistent.

What I Did

  • Cleaned: Removed obvious duplicates and normalized key fields for usability.
  • Categorized: Grouped leads by product interest and intended routing.
  • Distributed: Reassigned lists into the correct team/ownership buckets to enable immediate follow-up.

Impact

  • Faster follow-up after events.
  • Cleaner ownership and less confusion about who should work what.

What I Would Improve Next

  • More standardized intake format before lists are shared.
  • Repeatable checklist and routing rules applied the same way every time.
