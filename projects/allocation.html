Customer Allocation & Cross-Sell Governance

Ownership structure to reduce conflicts and prevent duplicate outreach across products.

Problem

  • Expansion and cross-sell outreach had unclear ownership, creating overlap and friction.
  • Contacts tied to active opportunities needed protection from unrelated outreach.

What I Built

  • Ownership tiers: Defined who can work which accounts and under what conditions.
  • Hold states: Temporary “holds” to prevent simultaneous outreach during handoffs or active work.
  • Cross-product boundaries: Allowed outreach into accounts while protecting contacts actively involved in other open opportunities.
  • Reassignment model: Clear path for moving ownership without losing visibility.

Impact

  • Reduced overlap and internal conflict during expansion workflows.
  • Created clearer expectations for allowable outreach behavior.

What I Would Improve Next

  • Better audit trail for changes (who changed what, when, why).
  • Automated detection for conflicting outreach when multiple opps exist.
