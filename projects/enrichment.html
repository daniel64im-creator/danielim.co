CRM Data Enrichment Initiative (~9,000 Accounts)

Large-scale firmographic cleanup and structured re-import to improve segmentation, targeting precision, and reporting integrity.

Context

  • Salesforce account base contained thousands of records with missing, inconsistent, or outdated firmographic fields.
  • Key fields affected: FTE, website domain, state, country, and time zone.
  • Inconsistent data reduced targeting precision and reporting reliability across SDR and AE workflows.

Structural Problems

  • Incomplete segmentation criteria for territory and vertical analysis.
  • Inaccurate time zone data affecting outreach timing.
  • Reporting dashboards reflecting inconsistent or null values.
  • No documented enrichment cadence or QA process.

Architecture Built

  • Population Export: Pulled full account dataset requiring cleanup.
  • Field Standardization: Normalized FTE ranges, standardized website formatting, corrected state/country naming conventions.
  • ID-Based Mapping: All updates keyed strictly by Salesforce Account ID to eliminate mismatch risk.
  • Structured Re-Import: Coordinated controlled upload with IT to preserve data integrity and avoid overwrite conflicts.
  • Pre-Import QA: Reviewed subsets for formatting consistency and duplicate risk prior to upload.

Governance & Controls

  • Strict ID-based updates prevented record misalignment.
  • Manual validation before import reduced systemic overwrite risk.
  • Structured coordination prevented field-level corruption.

Impact

  • Improved segmentation accuracy across ~9,000 accounts.
  • Increased confidence in territory, vertical, and size-based reporting.
  • Reduced SDR friction caused by missing firmographic context.
  • Strengthened CRM as a reliable operational source of truth.

Next Improvements

  • Implement recurring enrichment cadence (quarterly refresh).
  • Introduce lightweight QA sampling dashboard.
  • Automate validation checks for required firmographic fields.
