Real-Time Demo Booking Notifications

Event-triggered visibility system to notify stakeholders when demos are booked without requiring manual monitoring.

Context

  • Demos were being booked regularly but leadership lacked immediate visibility.
  • Stakeholders relied on manual dashboard checks or Slack follow-ups.
  • Over-notification risk could reduce signal quality.

Structural Problems

  • No automated alerting layer tied to confirmed demo bookings.
  • Manual monitoring introduced delays and inconsistency.
  • Unfiltered notifications would create operational noise.

Architecture Built

  • Event-Triggered Logic: Automation activated when a defined “demo booked” condition was met.
  • Scoped Filtering: Limited to specific record types and conditions to avoid noise.
  • Targeted Recipients: Notifications sent only to relevant stakeholders.
  • Signal Control: Structured logic prevented duplicate or irrelevant alerts.

Governance & Controls

  • Conditional logic enforced strict qualification of what constitutes a valid demo booking.
  • Controlled distribution reduced unnecessary visibility spam.
  • Aligned notification trigger directly to operational milestone.

Impact

  • Improved real-time visibility into booked demos.
  • Reduced reliance on manual monitoring.
  • Increased transparency for leadership without increasing noise.
  • Strengthened alignment between SDR activity and management awareness.

Next Improvements

  • Add simple reporting on notification volume and coverage.
  • Implement duplicate detection logic for reschedules.
  • Introduce exception tracking for missed trigger scenarios.
