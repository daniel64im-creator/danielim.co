Real-Time Demo Booking Notifications

Visibility system to notify stakeholders when demos are booked without requiring manual monitoring.

Problem

  • Demos were being booked, but leadership/stakeholders lacked immediate visibility unless they checked manually.
  • Over-notifying would create noise and reduce usefulness.

What I Built

  • Event-triggered notification logic: Sends notifications when the right “demo booked” signal is created.
  • Scoped filtering: Limited to specific record types/conditions to avoid noise.
  • Operational goal: Fast visibility with controlled volume.

Impact

  • Improved operational visibility into demo activity.
  • Reduced manual checking and “did we book anything?” ambiguity.

What I Would Improve Next

  • Add simple reporting on notification volume and coverage.
  • Define exception handling for duplicates or reschedules.
