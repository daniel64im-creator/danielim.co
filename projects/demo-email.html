Salesforce Demo Email Capture Automation

Automation to ensure demo attendee email lands in the Opportunity field required for marketing sync.

Problem

  • Demo attendee email started on the Lead, moved after conversion, and was not reliably present where marketing needed it.
  • This created missed or inconsistent downstream enrollment for post-demo workflows.

What I Built

  • Record-triggered Flow: Triggered by creation of the “Client Demo” event.
  • Data movement: Copied Contact email into the Opportunity field used for HubSpot sync.
  • Scope control: Limited to the relevant event type to avoid unintended updates.

Impact

  • Reduced manual copying and lowered risk of missing marketing enrollment.
  • Improved consistency of a key field used for reporting and marketing alignment.

What I Would Improve Next

  • Add validation/alerts for edge cases (missing email, multiple attendees, mismatched records).
  • Track coverage with a simple report to monitor failures/exceptions.
