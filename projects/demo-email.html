Salesforce Demo Email Capture Automation

Record-triggered automation to ensure demo attendee email is reliably captured at the Opportunity level for downstream marketing sync.

Context

  • Demo attendee email initially lived on the Lead record.
  • After Lead conversion, the required email field was not consistently present on the Opportunity.
  • Marketing automation relied on this Opportunity-level field for post-demo enrollment.

Structural Problems

  • Manual copying of email fields created inconsistency.
  • Missed updates resulted in incomplete marketing enrollment.
  • No enforcement layer guaranteed the required field was populated.

Architecture Built

  • Record-Triggered Flow: Activated upon creation of a “Client Demo” event.
  • Scoped Trigger Logic: Limited execution strictly to relevant event type to prevent unintended updates.
  • Field Mapping: Copied the primary Contact email into the Opportunity-level field used for HubSpot sync.
  • Automation-Based Enforcement: Removed dependency on manual SDR updates.

Governance & Controls

  • Conditional logic restricted execution to intended workflow.
  • Centralized required marketing field at Opportunity object level.
  • Reduced human error in high-frequency demo scheduling process.

Impact

  • Improved consistency of marketing enrollment after demos.
  • Reduced manual copying effort for SDRs.
  • Increased reliability of reporting tied to demo engagement.
  • Strengthened CRM-to-marketing system alignment.

Next Improvements

  • Add validation rule for missing or null Contact email.
  • Introduce monitoring report to flag automation exceptions.
  • Extend logic to handle multi-attendee demo edge cases.
