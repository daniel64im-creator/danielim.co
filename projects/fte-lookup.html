Instant FTE Lookup for SDR Prospecting

Fast-reference enrichment tool that surfaced institution size (FTE) during targeting without leaving the workflow.

Context

  • SDRs needed a quick way to gauge account size and priority.
  • FTE data existed externally but wasn’t always accessible at point of prospecting.

Structural Problems

  • Prospecting required time-consuming lookups across sources.
  • Prioritization was inconsistent without a shared size signal.
  • Repeated manual research created wasted time and variability.

Architecture Built

  • Lookup Workflow: Fast method to retrieve FTE for a target institution during prospecting.
  • Standardization: Consistent output format usable for prioritization and notes.
  • Operational Fit: Designed to reduce clicks and keep reps in motion.

Governance & Controls

  • Defined usage guidance so reps applied the signal consistently.
  • Reduced ad hoc assumptions about account size and priority.

Impact

  • Reduced time-to-context during prospecting.
  • Improved consistency of account prioritization.
  • Enabled faster targeting decisions without losing accuracy.

Next Improvements

  • Integrate into CRM fields to eliminate duplicate research.
  • Add QA checks for mismatched institution names.
  • Expand coverage beyond current dataset scope.
